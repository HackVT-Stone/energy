Capture and reuse practices like rain barrels and cisterns are designed to intercept and store runoff from impervious surfaces, such as rooftops. Rain barrels and cisterns can help to reduce stormwater runoff and protect water quality by capturing runoff on-site. The rooftop runoff captured in these vessels can be used for uses like landscape irrigation or vehicle washing—or the water can be slowly released to a lawn area, leaving the barrel or cistern ready to accept rain during the next storm. Underground cisterns can also be designed to allow for seeping release into the substrate to recharge groundwater.

This calculator uses default volumes of 55 gallons for rain barrels and 500 gallons for cisterns.
